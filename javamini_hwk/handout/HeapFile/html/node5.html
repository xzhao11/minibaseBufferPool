What to Turn In, and When

You should turn in copies of your code together with copies of the output produced by running the tests provided by the TAs. The assignment is due at 5PM on September 27th. The solution will be made public after that time, and solutions turned in after that time will not receive any credit. So be sure to turn in whatever you have, even if it is not working fully, at that time.

I emphasize that late submissions will not receive any credit. Computers0--and life!0--being what they are, expect everything to take longer than you expect, even taking this expectation into account. So start early, and plan on getting things done well before the due date. Nothing short of a nuclear explosion (in the CS building, not the South Pacific) constitutes a valid reason for an extension.
